Education & Investigation

We've developed alliances with Universities for mutual collaboration.

-University of the Republic of Uruguay - Chemical Engineer Faculty (UdeLar) Study program for Chemical Engineering career thesis. Main aspects: Research and experiments of cannabidiol (CBD) ethanol extraction.

-University of Buenos Aires, Argentina - Agronomy Faculty (UBA) Members of the Cannabis Study Group G.E.T. of the University, composed of teachers, graduates and students.

Main aspects: Collaborative investigation of cannabis production in Uruguay and Argentina.

-University of the Republic of Uruguay - Faculty of Biochemistry - Mycology (UdeLar) Financed by Basic Sciences Development Program from Uruguay (PEDEClBA) through the National Council for Innovation, Science and Technology (CONICYT).

Main aspects: Study of biological control for plagues and diseases in cannabis plants.
