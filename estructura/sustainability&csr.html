Sustainability, Quality and Corporate Social Responsibility:

Sustainability, Quality and Corporate Social Responsibility. We are a growing company in balance with nature. We care about the earth and people. That's why we develop our activities with the least environmental impact possible. We believe in circular economies and organic behaviors.

Certifications:

We do all our processes under the guidelines of the World Health organization and the European Medicine Agency.

We obtained the GACP certification (Good Agricultural and Collecting practices of medicinal plants).

We are in the process of an organic international certification, as well as a B company certification.

Natural supplies

We develop biocontrol using native microorganisms and we make our own compost.

Local relationships

We build alliances with local cooperatives as well as creating local employment and partnerships with local providers.

Recycling

We recycle at Campo Limpio, a national program for the collection and final destination of Phytosanitary products and fertilizers containers, where they also receive and recycle plastics, glass and cardboard.
